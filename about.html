CryptoSec.info is a simple website made to help beginners in the cryptocurrency-space by giving them sufficient knowledge on protecting their crypto investments, and learn about the risks they're taking when using certain wallets and or services.

Since I began to dig deep into bitcoin and cryptocurrencies in general somewhere in mid 2016, I couldn't even count how much people in social media sites are getting devastated due to them losing their investments due to hacks and scams. Some even losing as much as their whole life savings, some even losing their families. Hopefully creating this simple website would somewhat prevent these things to happen, even though just in a very small scale. I'd personally be happy enough if one of my articles would save a single person's investments.

This website has zero ads, but has referral links in the hopes of it covering the domain and hosting fees. So please consider using them.
